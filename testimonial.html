i love tom walsh! hire him!

Tom Walsh is extraordinary. One of the best production designers I've worked with.

He was with us on Longmire for 4 years and was one of the lynchpins of the series. We relied on him heavily. His sets are magnificent. The needs of storytelling drive his designs, and his worlds reflect a deep understanding of character. He has a great knowledge of the exigencies of production, and his sets are incredibly user friendly for the crew; there is space; walls and windows move and hinge; there are great opportunities for light; and there are many versatile angles for shooting. The spaces are also always rich with relevant detail. They reflect the love and care he puts into his work.

Equally important, he managed to pull off weekly miracles on a shoestring budget. I marveled at how far he was able to stretch his dollar. He is inventive and resourceful. A team-playing problem-solver who listens. Additionally, his department was one of the best managed on the show. He hires excellent people, communicates clearly with them, and delegates appropriately. He also has vast experience working in New Mexico - he knows the talent pool and the resources - which is critical.

Just describing him makes me miss him all over again. I am truly jealous of your opportunity to work with him. He is a wonderfully collaborative, responsible, and creative leader. He is additive in every way.


Very best,
Greer Shephard
Producer/Director/Executive @ The Shephard/Robin Company

Thomas A. Walsh, adg
production designer

Thomas’ diverse career spans feature films, IMAX, episodic series, documentaries, commercials, Broadway and regional theatre. The Production Designer of the popular Netflix series, Longmire, he originated the EPIX series, Graves and the Disney/ABC series Desperate Housewives. Nominated many times, Thomas is the winner of the Emmy Award for the CBS-TV series Buddy Faro. Feature film credits include The Handmaids Tale, A Gathering of Old Men and The Majestic. Thomas continues to work on Broadway recently designing the new American play, Annapurna. He designed the Tony Award winning production of Children of a Lesser God and the original critically acclaimed production of Luis Valdez’s Zoot Suit.
